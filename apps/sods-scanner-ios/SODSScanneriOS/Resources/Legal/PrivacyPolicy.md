# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 16, 2026_

SODS Scanner iOS processes local BLE and LAN discovery data on your device to provide scanner and visualization features.

## Data We Process

- BLE advertisement metadata from nearby devices.
- Local network host discovery data (IP, service, and device metadata).
- Imported database files you choose to load (OUI and BLE metadata text files).
- Subscription entitlement status provided by Apple StoreKit.

## Data Storage

- Scanner history and app settings are stored locally on device.
- Subscription entitlement cache is stored locally to support temporary offline grace.
- Imported metadata overrides are stored locally until reset.

## Data Sharing

- This app does not require a separate backend account to scan devices.
- Subscription billing and receipt verification are handled by Apple.
- We do not sell personal data.

## Permissions

- Bluetooth permission is used for BLE scanning.
- Local Network permission is used for LAN discovery.

If permissions are denied, related scanner capabilities are unavailable.

## Your Choices

- You can reset imported metadata overrides in the Databases tab.
- You can cancel subscriptions through Apple Subscriptions settings.
- You can uninstall the app to remove local data.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the support channel listed in the App Store product page.
